Colin Neumann Jr.

ConneR

ConneR or Colin Neumann Jr. is a main character from the mobile rhythm game Cytus II.

ConneR was born in Node 13, A city where almost all technology is banned. While growing up in Node 13 he didn't have a very good relation with his father, causing him to walk away and getting into illegal exploration and archeology in Node 13's forbidden area.

ConneR has makes frequent trips into the forbidden area. Almost getting killed during one, if it wasn't for the A.R.C. who picked him up and provided emergency medical care. Including replacing his right leg and eye. To great displeasure of ConneR.

throughout the overarching story ConneR keeps exploring the forbidden area and helps the other main characters. He also has his own story where he tries to decipher the function of and Ancient family artifact he has inherited.
