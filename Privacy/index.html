Privacy

When visiting and using this website the following data will be used and or stored[1]:

  • Your useragent.
  • The language of your browser.
  • A Unique User Identifier (UUID).
  • How many times you have visited this site.

[1] all this data will be stored in the localstorage of your browser and maybe sent to a webhook on Discord in my Discord server. (if you have GCP on only your useragent will be sent to the webhook.)
