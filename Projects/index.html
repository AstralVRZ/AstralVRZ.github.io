Minecraft Fandom Wiki redirect userscript

After watching PhoenixSC's latest video about the Minecraft fandom wiki, I decided to make a simple Tampermonkey userscript to redirect you from any article on the minecraft.fandom.com wiki to the article on minecraft.wiki.
This assumes that the path to the article on minecraft.wiki is the same as on the fandom wiki.

You can find the code on Github or as a Gist I personally use Tampermonkey, but it should work on similar extensions (maybe? idk tbh lol).
